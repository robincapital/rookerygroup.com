About

A Private Investment Vehicle Built on Local Knowledge

Rookery Management Group is a privately held real estate investment vehicle founded by Nick Diaz and Cong Vo. The fund focuses on the acquisition, rehabilitation, and development of residential and commercial properties across South Florida — a market both principals know intimately through decades of direct experience.

Rookery operates with a straightforward mandate: source opportunities that others overlook, apply disciplined underwriting, and execute with the operational expertise of an active owner-operator. The fund is not open to general solicitation. Opportunities are evaluated selectively through the principals' existing network of local relationships.

Principals

The People Behind the Fund

Nick Diaz
Co-Founder & Principal

Nick brings over twenty years of institutional financial markets experience to Rookery's investment committee and deal pricing process. He is the Founder and CIO of Robin Capital Group, a Miami Beach-based Registered Investment Advisor, and Co-Founder of Nezumia, a quantitative AI hedge fund. His analytical background in systematic risk management and capital allocation informs Rookery's disciplined approach to underwriting and deal evaluation.

Cong Vo
Co-Founder & Managing Principal

Cong is a Miami native with a career spanning decades of hands-on real estate rehabilitation and development across South Florida. He manages the entirety of Rookery's operations — from deal origination and construction oversight to investor relations and asset management. Through his independent accounting practice, CVO Financial, Cong brings both financial rigor and an unmatched network of local relationships that continuously surface new investment opportunities.
